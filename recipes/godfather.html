Godfather

"Godfather cocktail" by Stuart Webster from Southampton, England is licensed under CC BY 2.0 .

In the same style as the Old Fashioned, the Godfather is another simple - but delicious and flexible - classic cocktail.

The delight of a Godfather lies in the balance between smoky, malty scotch whisky, and sweet, nutty amaretto. This balance point might change depending on your choice of whisky - so don't be afraid to experiment with the ratio!

Ingredients

  • 2 oz blended Scotch whisky
  • 0.25-0.5 oz Amaretto

Steps

  1. Pour amaretto into your glass.
  2. Pour whisky into your glass.
  3. Add a good amount of ice. Stir.
  4. Serve.

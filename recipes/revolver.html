Revolver

"Old Fashioned cocktail at Kogo" by Bex.Walton is licensed under CC BY 2.0 .

Another riff on the Old Fashioned, the Revolver will take you back to the tastes and aromas of the Old West.

Pairing bourbon and coffee liqueur on their own would end up a touch sweet - so the Revolver balances these out with the addition of astringent orange bitters.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz bourbon whiskey
  • 0.5 oz coffee liqueur
  • 2 dashes orange bitters
  • 1 swathe orange peel

Steps

  1. Prepare your garnish. Use a peel to remove a piece of skin from an orange. Take care to remove as little pith - the white stuff - as possible.
  2. Add coffee liqueur, bourbon, and orange bitters to a mixing glass.
  3. Add ice, and stir until well-chilled.
  4. Strain into a coupe or a Nick and Nora glass.
  5. With a lighter or lit match, squeeze the orange peel's oils over the cocktail, through the flame.
  6. Garnish with the peel.
  7. Serve!
